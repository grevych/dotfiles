
POWERLINE
--------
Install font patch (git repo is added to restoreVim.sh)
iTerm2 -> Preferences > Profiles > Text > NON ASCII > Go Mono for Powerline
iTerm2 -> Preferences > Profiles > Color Preset > Arthur | AdventureTime | Afterglow

iTerm2SchemeColor repository is important
